Basic gameplay controls for the avaliable characters:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

  • BrazSonic:<\/strong>
    Spindash (down + 1\/2\/3 button); Flying (1\/2\/3 button in the air); Super Peel Out (hold up + 1\/2\/3 button and release a little later)<\/li>
  • PeloTails: <\/strong>
    Spindash (down + button 1\/2\/3); Hover Lasso (button 1\/2\/3 mid air repeatedly)<\/li>
  • Bahianuckles:<\/strong>
    Spindash (down + button 1\/2\/3); Glide (button 1\/2\/3 mid air); Climb (hit a wall while gliding, and hold up\/down to move vertically)<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    Check below some unlockables:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    • Free Play:<\/strong>
      For unlocking, finish the Story Mode once.
      After finishing, on the main menu, the Story mode option will be blocked for good and then “Free Play” will be avaliable. There you will be able to play all the Story mode levels, regular levels and bosses, in order to collect more coins, stars, lives, points. Therefore you can replay them as different characters, for a different experience. <\/li>
    • Multiplayer 4 player (PC Version Only)<\/strong>
      Finish the Story Mode once.<\/li>
    • Bubsy:<\/strong>
      Finish the Story Mode once.
      Skills: <\/strong>Glide (button 1\/2\/3 mid air) , Spin attack (hold down while moving), Super Peel Out (hold up + 1\/2\/3 button and release a little later) <\/li>
    • Sonic: <\/strong>
      Take all the 3 emeralds from the DEMO.
      Skills: <\/strong>homing attack ( button 1 or 3 mid air, close to whatever enemy), Stomp (down on mid air), Boost (hold button 2).<\/li>
    • Guimo:<\/strong>
      Take all the stars from the regular levels (Free Play).
      Skills: <\/strong>double jump (button 1 or 3 mid air), shoot (button 2). Regarding the shooting, the shots became stronger after 25 coins and even more by collecting elemental power ups (water, fire and thunder), each one with different ranges.<\/li>
    • Classic BrazSonic:<\/strong>
      Take all the extra levels stars.
      Skills: <\/strong>Spindash (down + 1\/2\/3 button) , dropdash(hold button 1\/2\/3 mid air until reaching the floor).<\/li>
    • BraSonic Gallery:<\/strong>
      Unlock everything of the DEMO.<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n
