The game plot happens on Brazil, in the year 20XX (some year of 21th century). Thanks to a initiative promoted by the lunatic congressman Ivo Corruptnik<\/strong>, leads and sends an huge army of armed robots, called Corruptbots<\/strong>, to sweep the corruption, criminals and every nation oppositiors away.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Actually, those war machines, instead of fighting the crime, they started to attacking citizens, robbing and just bringing the destruction to the places.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

All this war is only a smoke curtain to disguise their quest to find the legendary 7 Corruption Emeralds<\/strong>, magic gems, of unknown origin, when gathered can give an extreme power for its owner.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

And the rest of the government does not seem to be worried about this situation…<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Among this crisis, BrazSonic<\/strong>, the protagonist, comes right just after a fight with random bullies to another against the Corruptbots.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

After beating them without difficulties, he sees that little animals<\/strong> (birds, puppies, capybaras and others) comes out from their scraps, what means that the critters had been hostages by the bots. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
