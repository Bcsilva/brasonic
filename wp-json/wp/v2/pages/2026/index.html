BrazSonic 20XX is a solo project where, even using existing materials such as code snippets and extensions, sound, music, and digital art, I do everything else from scratch: stages, bosses, animations, gameplay, stories (cutscenes), manage social networks, record release videos, etc., etc. It’s cool and rewarding, yes, but it’s kind of tiring and takes more time than I wish it didn’t.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Now I am opening up new possibilities to form a group of supporters for the BrazSonic 20XX project, with different forms of contribution:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Beta Testing<\/h4>\n\n\n\n

Test game builds, especially the DEMO version before it is released to the public. This avoids the wear and tear of excessive uploads of the game builds.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Translations: English and Spanish<\/h4>\n\n\n\n

The BrazSonic 20XX DEMO already has translations in English and Spanish. However, the Spanish translation was done in a rush, with the help of Google Translate. Despite its efficiency, it was not very good at translating particular stuff like popular idioms, slangs, and even memes. The final version will have a lot more text in the stories and I will need help with that, both English and Spanish.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Sprites<\/h4>\n\n\n\n

Animations for NPCs (non-playable characters): random people, enemies (humans and robots), items, system screens, sceneries details, in pixel art, 16 colors.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Backgrounds<\/h4>\n\n\n\n

Backgrounds for the game levels, in pixel art, 16 colors<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Soundtrack<\/h4>\n\n\n\n

BrazSonic 20XX currently has tracks made by several artists, remixed from original tracks from various Sonic games. I’m looking for someone who knows how to remix songs in MIDI format to 16 bit Mega Drive style.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Illustration and 2D Animation<\/h4>\n\n\n\n

In some moments of the stories that happen in the game, they will have animated scenes, of conversation (like that post-S\u00e3o Paulo Zone scene where BrazSonic has a transmission with the game antagonist) and totally animated (I even have drafts of an full animated intro for BrazSonic 20XX). The illustration work will also be very useful for the promotional art of the game, showing some situations between levels, concept art, characters in different poses, game characters celebrating holidays (for social networks), etc. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Videos<\/h4>\n\n\n\n

Video recording of gameplays from the game.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Promoting on Social Media<\/h4>\n\n\n\n

For game channel influencers, the promoting BrazSonic is very important, as it is a Brazilian indie game made as a hobby, for entertainment and free speech.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Necessidade de novos seguidores<\/h4>\n\n\n\n

For Twitter<\/a>, Facebook<\/a> and especially Youtube<\/a>. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

\n\n\n\n

Okay, but what do I get out of it?<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Because it is a fangame (fan game), which uses the image of the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise, IP from SEGA (TM), as well as having the presence of her characters, BrazSonic is a free game, without the intention of to make something commercial, that is, I don’t gain anything from it, I just use it as a hobby and technical portfolio. This is already mentioned even in Terms of Use<\/a>. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

What I can offer is exchange and partnerships in the same way that I am proposing here with your work as well. This contribution with BrazSonic can not only be good for your artistic and technical portfolio but also aims to form a closer and more engaged community via the Internet. Your name will also be in the BrazSonic 20XX credits<\/a>!<\/p>\n\n\n\n
