Unfortunately, due to some significant issues such as lack of communication, determination, and presence I had to stop the BrazSonic 2 project for good. Although, all these years of work were worth where I could learn a lot of stuff to share. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

What made you do that?<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

The reason I’m writing and divulging all these reports is precisely to share stories and experiences, which can bring some usefulness to any kind of person, not just professionals who deal with programming and projects.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I also spent several years without giving any news about the progress of BrazSonic 2, which inevitably caused many to lose interest. The purpose of this material is to fill the emptiness left for all this time, with information not previously disclosed, curiosities, screens, videos and downloads of older playable builds.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I separated the stories here for periods, where each of them had points that marked the development of BrazSonic 2 irreversibly, for good and bad. This way of separating content in “sub-pages” was due to the reason that there’s a lot of images and videos among the text and downloadable media.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
