What are the game controls?<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

Keyboard:
Movement: arrows
Jump: space
Shoot\/boost (other characters): Ctrl\/Shift
Pause: Enter<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Bindable gamepad in PC and Android versions, with X-input (modern gamepads) and Direct Input (old gamepads).<\/p>\n\n\n\n

How to bind gamepad buttons?<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

In the main options screen, go to settings -> configure controls. Then you will be taken to a new screen with “4 BrazSonics”. There, using the mouse cursor, you can configure up to 4 different gamepads and test them immediately.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

How about the Mobile version controls?<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

In the Android version, you can toggle between the Touchscreen and Gamepad control modes. Regarding the gamepad, you can configure them like in the PC version, but it requires a properly connected Bluetooth gamepad on your device before running the game.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

How about the English and Spanish translations?<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

These are already available in-game. They might still have some translation inconsistencies, which are going to be solved in bugfixes from future build releases.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When the final version of BrazSonic 20XX will be released?<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

For now, I estimate a little before or in the middle of 2020, if all goes well, of course!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Is BrazSonic 20XX a sequel from the original BrazSonic?<\/p>\n\n\n\n

No, the BrazSonic universe is definitely reset.
After the BrazSonic 2 being canceled (full story here<\/a>), everything that was supposed to happen, in the plot, after the BrazSonic 1 facts are not going to happen anymore. BrazSonic 20XX is a new beginning, a new generation.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
