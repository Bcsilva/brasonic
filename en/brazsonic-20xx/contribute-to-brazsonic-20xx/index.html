Contribute to BrazSonic 20XX!

27 de May de 202027 de May de 2020

Among all this pandemic, I made great strides in the BrazSonic 20XX project, where I was able to better polish the game, making the experience of playing it increasingly accessible and enjoyable, along with bug fixes, lags (especially in the mobile version) ), significant implementations (new physics) and more.

All of this only in the DEMO version, which was released at the end of last year and has now been re-released with a major improvement.

The final version of the game is scheduled to be released between the second semester (starting in August) and December 20XX. We have a lot in advance, but to meet this deadline, it’s giving me a lot of work.

In this I decided to open up possibilities to have even more people contributing to the project, in several ways:

  • Beta Testing
  • Translations
  • Sprites and Backgrounds (pixel-art)
  • Illustration
  • 2D Animation
  • videos
  • Disclosure on social networks
  • New followers (especially for Youtube Channel)
