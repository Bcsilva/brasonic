Welcome to brasonic.com, the official website for BrazSonic (aka BraSonic)..

BrazSonic (aka BraSonic in Latin languages) is a 2d sidescroller fangame(a fan-tribute game) released for PC/Windows released in 2004, as a parody of Sonic the Hedgehog and Brazil. In the game, you play as BrazSonic, a Brazilian version of the blue hedgehog, who has to run across the many Brazilian places and face challenges at high speed!

Now BrazSonic is back on a new brand adventure, as BrazSonic 20XX, a new game with a playable DEMO for PC(Windows) and Android!
