BrazSonic 2 – 2010 to 2013, and beyond

At that time I did not release more demos and I moved away from the communities because of the work and change of area, but I worked gradually in BrazSonic 2 and in a possible reboot of the first game.

Regarding BrazSonic 2, I thought about creating a new villain, who remembered the villain Dr. Eggman (Robotnik), but I did not create a name, he would be a Robotnik with a green suit and a little more hair than the enemy of Sonic. The phases also would have robots like those of the games of Sonic, that would be part of the army of the corrupt villain. In future he would be named Dr. Corruptnik (see BrazSonic 20XX).

The plot:

On a normal day in Rio de Janeiro, the sun is covered by an immense shadow. From this figure emerges a red-eye, which begins to shoot destructive lasers rays against the city, causing general panic. By far BrazSonic watches the chaos established in the city, and with the legendary $100 banknotes in hand (from the first game), leaves to save the city in its “super form.”

After the battle against the threat discovered, BrazSonic descends from the heights and everyone applauds him. Your 15 minutes of fame were guaranteed! But the legendary banknotes were vanished forever, thus making him return to normal soon after.

The next morning, in a rest by the beaches of Rio, BrazSonic and his friends once again witness an attack against the city, only this time the focus comes from the hill. Soon the heroes leave in search of the new threat.

After Rio Zone Act 2, after defeating the Christ Redeemer, which takes off into the sky, a little further on, people are gathered watching a supposed statement on TV. The statement comes from an unknown deputy, who claims to be the new president of Brazil. His words sound rather aggressive, as if he were willing to start a dictatorship, and states that already an army of war robots to end supposed terrorists.

The heroes became worried, while the people remain indifferent. As the story goes, they soon discover that they are the targets of that deputy.

Stages

Rio Zone

Corrected bugs and implementation of a phase between speed-focused act 1 and 2, where the player would run by the beach, raise the Pão de Açucar by the tram, and then pass through the city again. The soundtrack of this phase I used an instrumental from the band Autoramas.

Cubatão Zone

Boss of the stage would be a giant robot crab.

Sampa Zone

Perhaps the most complex stage of the game. At the end of Act 1, I came to implement a part where the player would be chased by a subway at high speed while sliding along the rails, is a part with several loopings and obstacles that demand rapid reflexes.

Minas Zone

The first act would be focused on speed, only with a view from above, with 3d touches on the viaducts and other moving elements of the screen, and the gravity of the character to jump obstacles. Part 2 would be in an inner-city terrified by robot-induced earthquakes, where the player would eventually discover an underground mining station full of deadly machines. The layout that inspired me of phase was the Marble Garden of Sonic 3, very vast and full of alternative paths.

Varginha Zone

I got to set up this level, with almost-done sceneries, but then I decided to delete it for lack of connection with the plot of the game.

Salvador Zone

Now there would be an act before the original, with a more beach-oriented and underwater course, as well as Sonic 2’s Aquatic Ruins Zone. I ended up finishing 2nd, with the Lacerda Lift right in the middle of the stage.

Sertão Zone

Layout ok, and with junk dump parts. In act 3, the heroes invade an aerial armada of the villain, but they get caught.

Chuí Zone

Remaining in the game, it would be a level away from the south edge of the mainland, where the heroes would have to flee a maximum-security prison.

Pelotas zone

Initially, a very colorful action level to break the normative mood of the other phases, but it was getting too fanciful, in addition to having a design, although turned to the skill of the Pelotails tie, very frustrating to get through. So I decided to make a more linear and less difficult version.

Iguaçu Zone

No change in the first two acts. Only in the third, which had been removed, and then began to be redone from scratch.

Pantanal Zone

Phase with a suspense climate in the air, with dangers like alligators, piranhas, malaria mosquitoes and also a giant anaconda.

Mato Grosso Zone

I started to do it, but I did not go much further.

Belém Zone

It reminds a little of Launch Base of Sonic 2. The stage would happen in the port Vero Peso harbor, of the city of Belém – PA.

Ama Zone

Bug fixes, both slightly functional acts.

Acre Zone

Act 1 takes place on a military base that hides underground access with military enemies, acts the same as before and acts as an underground base full of paths restricted to the type of character you play.

Brasília Zone

Not done
